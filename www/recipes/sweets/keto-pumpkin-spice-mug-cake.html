Keto Pumpkin Spice Mug Cake

Enjoy a quick, easy, and delicious coconut flour keto vanilla berry mug cake in less than 5 minutes. This decadent treat has hints of cheesecake flavor, especially noticable with the variation free of egg whites. Feel free to include different berry varieties depending on your mood. Some of my favorites include frozen blueberries, strawberries, raspberries, blackberries, and cherries!

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp butter, melted
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tbsp Sour Cream
  • 2 tbsp Almond Flour
  • 1 tbsp coconut flour
  • 2 tbsp granulated sweetener of choice or more to taste
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp Pumpkin Spice
  • ½ tsp Cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp Nutmeg

Instructions

  1. Melt butter.
  2. Whisk the egg separately.
  3. Stir all ingredients together until all lumps are gone.
  4. Microwave on HIGH for 1 minute and 30 seconds.

Pumpkin Spice Maple Cream Layered Mug Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • ¼ cup heavy cream
  • ⅓ cup pumpkin puree not pie filling
  • ¼ cup coconut flour
  • ⅓ cup Swerve sweetener
  • ¼ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp pumpkin pie spice (or use ¼ tsp cinnamon & ¼ tsp cloves)
  • pinch salt
  • Maple Cream Frosting

Instructions

  1. Whisk eggs, heavy cream, and pumpkin puree together until combined.
  2. Stir in remaining ingredients.
  3. Grease 2 ramekins. I used quiche ramekins, more shallow.
  4. Divided batter among 2 serving dishes.
  5. Microwave one cake at a time 1 - 2 minutes or until toothpick in center comes out clean.
  6. Alternatively, bake these in a preheated oven at 350 degrees for 15 minutes.
  7. Allow to cool completely before frosting.

Notes

Prep Time: 3 minutes
Cook Time: 2 minutes
Total Time: 5 minutes
Servings: 4
Calories: 242 kcal
Net Carbs: 4 grams
