Sugar-Free Pumpkin Spice Maple Cream Layered Mug Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • ¼ cup heavy cream
  • ⅓ cup pumpkin puree not pie filling
  • ¼ cup coconut flour
  • ⅓ cup Swerve sweetener
  • ¼ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp pumpkin pie spice (or use ¼ tsp cinnamon & ¼ tsp cloves)
  • pinch salt
  • Maple Cream Frosting

Instructions

  1. Whisk eggs, heavy cream, and pumpkin puree together until combined.
  2. Stir in remaining ingredients.
  3. Grease 2 ramekins. I used quiche ramekins, more shallow.
  4. Divided batter among 2 serving dishes.
  5. Microwave one cake at a time 1 - 2 minutes or until toothpick in center comes out clean.
  6. Alternatively, bake these in a preheated oven at 350 degrees for 15 minutes.
  7. Allow to cool completely before frosting.

Notes

Prep Time: 3 minutes
Cook Time: 2 minutes
Total Time: 5 minutes
Servings: 4
Calories: 242 kcal
Net Carbs: 4 grams
