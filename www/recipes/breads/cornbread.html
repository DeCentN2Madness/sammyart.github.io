Yogurt honey butter sweet potato cornbread

Procured from a Facebook post from Off Grid Living, Homesteading and Farming group.

Ingredients:
- 1 cup mashed sweet potatoes 🍠
- 1 cup yellow cornmeal 🌽
- 1 cup all-purpose flour 🍞
- 1/4 cup honey 🍯
- 1 tablespoon baking powder 🥄
- 1/2 teaspoon salt 🧂
- 4 large egg yolks 🥚
- 1/2 cup melted butter 🧈
- 1 cup plain yogurt (instead of buttermilk) 🥛
Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C) 🔥.
2. In a bowl, combine all the dry ingredients: cornmeal, flour, baking powder, and salt 🥄.
3. In a separate bowl, mix together the mashed sweet potatoes, eggs, melted butter, and plain yogurt until smooth 🍠🥛.
4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry mixture and stir until just combined. Transfer the batter to a baking dish and bake for 25-30 minutes or until golden 🍞.
5. Serve warm. 🍽️
Tips and Options:
- Consider adding a teaspoon of cinnamon or nutmeg to enhance the flavor 🌰.
- Substitute half of the all-purpose flour with whole wheat flour for a healthier version 🌾.
- For a spicy twist, mix in diced jalapeños or chopped green chiles 🌶️.
- This cornbread is great as a side dish for chili or paired with a holiday feast!
