Wish-Bone Salad Dressing

An international ad campaign, including both print and TV ads, showcases Barbara's unique illustrated style. A combination of eastern and western imagery and philosophy are merged to create a vision of peaceful, conscious living and integrity. A greater good expression of fashion, which marries philosophy with sensitive imagery, generating a vision of peaceful utopia and conscious living.
