Barbara Samanich

Designer / Illustrator / Director

Sam is a multi disciplinary designer based in Tempe, Arizona specializing in brand identity design, art direction and design for advertising. She brings with her experience gained working on major international brands Aramark, Kraft, Lipton, Wish-Bone, Starbucks, Saks Fifth Avenue and Einstein Bros among many more.

International ad campaigns featuring both print and TV ads showcased Sam's unique illustrative style. In conjunction with international ad agencies and the art direction of Lowe Lintas and Ammirati Puris Lintas, Barbara's work launched a successful and productive series of advertisements. With its proven result of increasing sales, the campaign ran for seven remarkable years. This signature look was created by combining Sam's playful, unique style and her hand drawn calligraphy, merged with clever concepts, headlines, tag lines, and copy writing.

In 2002, Sam co founded the boutique apparel design company Samamama, which gained acclaim
