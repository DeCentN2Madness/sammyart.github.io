Gaps Diet Friendly Carrot Cake

With ingredients friendly to the GAPS diet, this carrot cake has also been modified to have a lower glycemic index for those seeking to retain a state of ketosis. Just be careful not to over-indulge, as this cake still packs a caloric punch.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups blanched almond flour (not meal)
  • 1 tsp Celtic sea salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tbsp ground cinammon
  • 1 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 5 egg yolks
  • 3 ripe bananas
  • ½ cup honey
  • ¼ cup coconut oil
  • 3 cups grated carrot
  • ½ cup golden raisins
  • ⅔ cup chopped walnuts

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine almond flour, salt, baking soda, cinammon and nutmeg.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix together egg yolks, bananas, honey, and oil.
  3. Stir carrots, raisins, and walnuts into wet ingredients.
  4. Stir wet ingredients into dry.
  5. Transfer batter into two well-greased 9-inch cake pans.
  6. Bake at 325℉ for 35 minutes.
  7. Cool cake for 3 hours, then remove from pans.
  8. Frost with cream cheese frosting.
