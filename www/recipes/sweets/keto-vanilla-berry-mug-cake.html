Keto Vanilla Berry Mug Cake

Enjoy a quick, easy, and delicious coconut flour keto vanilla berry mug cake in less than 5 minutes. This decadent treat has hints of cheesecake flavor, especially noticable with the variation free of egg whites. Feel free to include different berry varieties depending on your mood. Some of my favorites include frozen blueberries, strawberries, raspberries, blackberries, and cherries!

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp butter melted
  • 2 tbsp cream cheese full fat
  • 2 tbsp coconut flour
  • 1 tbsp granulated sweetener of choice or more to taste
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ¼ tsp baking powder
  • 1 egg medium
  • 2½ tbsp frozen berries

Instructions

  1. Place butter and cream cheese in a microwavable mug or ramekin and microwave on HIGH for 20 seconds.
  2. Add coconut flour, sweetener, vanilla, and baking powder then mix well. Add the egg and mix again
  3. Scrape down the sides of the mug, then press in 6 frozen raspberries into the cake batter.
  4. Microwave on HIGH for 1 minute and 20 seconds.

Notes

You can reduce the total carbs further by omitting the berries to make a simple keto vanilla mug cake.

This mug cake can be made without egg white by using 2 egg yolks instead of the single whole egg. This will result in a consistency more like a dense bread pudding or mug pancake.

Please adjust microwave cooking times based upon the strength of your microwave. Most standard microwaves require 1:20 as stated in the recipe, though more powerful microwaves may only need 1 minute.

No microwave? No problem. Simply bake at 180C/350F in a cupcake case or muffin tray for 12 minutes.
