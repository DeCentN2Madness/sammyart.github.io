Keto Cream Cheese Cake Topping

Whether using honey, erythritol, monk fruit extract, or a combination, this cream cheese frosting recipe brings home the flavor.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces (16 tbsp) cream cheese
  • 4 tbsp (2 oz) unsalted butter
  • 1 cup (8 oz) chilled whipping cream
  • ½ cup (8 tbsp) sweetener (powdered erythritol/monk fruit granules OR honey)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Bring butter & cream cheese to room temp.
  2. Whip cream until peaks form.
  3. In separate bowl, beat butter, cream cheese, sweetener, & vanilla together until smooth & creamy.
  4. Stir whipped cream into cream cheese mixture with mixer until proper thickness.

Vanilla Cream Cheese Frosting

This frosting works great on a carrot cake, but works very well on nearly any mug cake.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces cream cheese (softened)
  • ¼ cup Swerve Confectioners
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ¼ tsp vanilla liquid stevia
  • 2 tbsp heavy cream or 3 tbsp depending on consistency you need

Instructions

  1. Using a stand mixer or hand held electric mixer, place cream cheese, confectioners, vanilla and stevia in bowl and mix until the cream cheese is smooth.
  2. Add one tablespoon of heavy cream at a time, blend until a smooth consistency appears after each tablespoon.
  3. Refrigerate for 2 - 3 hours or overnight which will allow it to thicken in texture.
  4. When ready to decorate, spoon into a pastry bag or plastic bag with a snipped corner to decorate or simply spread onto cake or cupcakes with a butter knife.
  5. Keep refrigerated.

Notes

Prep Time 5 minutes
Servings 8 servings @2 tbsp each
Calories 116 kcal
Net Carbs 1 g
