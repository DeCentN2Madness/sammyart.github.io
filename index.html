Barbara Samanich

Designer / Illustrator / Director

Sam is a multi disciplinary designer based in Tempe, Arizona specializing in brand identity design, art direction and design for advertising. She brings with her experience gained working on major international brands Aramark, Kraft, Lipton, Wish-Bone, Starbucks, Saks Fifth Avenue and Einstein Bros among many more.

International ad campaigns featuring both print and TV ads showcasing Sam's illustrative style ran for seven record breaking years due to a proven ability to increase product sales. The signature look was created by combining Sam's uniquely playful style with her own hand drawn calligraphic lettering, merged with clever concepts, headlines, tag lines, and copy writing.
