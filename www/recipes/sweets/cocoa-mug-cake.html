KETO COCOA MUG CAKE MIX

Version 1

yield: 1 prep time: 5 MINUTES total time: 5 MINUTES

If you are looking for an easy and inexpensive gift to give this holiday season, check out this homemade keto mug cake mix. Pair it with a nice mug and a couple bottles of Torani syrups and you have a great low carb gift in minutes!

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup almond flour
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon confectioners sweetener
  • 2 tablespoons Torani sugar free syrup
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Instructions

To make the mix: In a large bowl or food processor, add the almond flour, baking powder, sweetener and cocoa powder. Using the food processor will ensure that it blends well and doesn't have any lumps.

NOTE: You will probably want to triple or quadruple this recipe to fit your jar. The amounts in this recipe will only make 1 mug cake. To make 1 mug cake from a large jar of mix:

  1. Add 1 tablespoon of butter to your mug and microwave for 30 seconds to melt. Then swirl the butter around the mug to essentially grease the sides so the mug cake won't stick.
  2. Add 6 tablespoons of the mug cake mix, 1 egg yolk and 2 tablespoons of your favorite Torani sugar free syrup. Mix well to combine.
  3. Microwave for 30-60 seconds until the cake is done. Don't over cook or it will get dry.

Notes

This recipe will make 1 mug cake. If you just want to make the mug cake for yourself, follow the directions and instead of adding 6 tablespoons of the mix, add all of the ingredients listed above.

If you are giving this as a gift, you may want to double or quadruple the mix to fit in your jar.

Cooking times will vary depending on the size of the mug you are using.

338 cals / 28.9g fat / 10.6g carbs / 8.7g fiber / 9.8g protein = 1.9g net carbs

Version 2

This healthy low carb chocolate mug cake is moist, fluffy and ready in less than a minute. Satisfy your sweet tooth with this protein packed recipe! It's also naturally gluten free, vegan, paleo, dairy free and sugar free recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 scoop protein powder (32-34 grams)
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 T coconut flour
  • 1 T granulated sweetener of choice*
  • 1 T cocoa powder
  • 1 large egg OR 1/4 cup liquid egg whites (see notes for Vegan option)
  • 1/4 cup milk of choice (I used unsweetened almond)
  • Chocolate chips to top (optional)

Instructions

  1. Grease a microwave safe bowl with cooking spray and add the protein powder, baking powder, coconut flour, sweetener of choice, cocoa powder and mix well.
  2. Add the egg/whites and mix into the dry mixture.
  3. Add the milk of choice- If batter is too crumbly, continue adding milk of choice until a very thick batter is formed.
  4. Top with chocolate chips and microwave for 60 seconds, or until just cooked in the centre.

Oven Option

Follow as above, but bake in the oven for 8-15 minutes, depending on consistency desired- Mug cake is cooked once a toothpick comes out 'just' clean from the center.

Notes

  • * I omitted the sweetener as my protein powder was sweetened
  • For the vegan option, you won't need any flax eggs/egg substitute- Simply omit and slowly add milk of choice until a thick batter is formed. Microwave/bake until 'just' cooked in the center.
