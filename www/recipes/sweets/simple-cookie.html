Simple Cookie is Simply Delicious

This cookie is vegan, keto, grain-free, and egg-free!

Ingredients

  • 2 c almond flour (fine, blanched)
  • 4 tbsp sugar equivalent (monkfruit granules highly recommended!)
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 4 tbsp water

Instructions

  1. mix ingredients together
  2. form into balls (about 10)
  3. smash balls onto well-buttered cookie sheet
  4. bake @ 350℉ for 14 minutes

Notes

This cookie can be modified in many ways to produce simply amazing results. Mix and match the below additions; don't be shy!

  • ½ c chocolate chips
  • ½ tbsp vanilla
