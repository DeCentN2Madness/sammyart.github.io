Low Carb Carrot Cake Mug Cake

This great recipe may not be entirely carb-free due to the carrot, but with the cream cheese icing, you won't give a damn!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • ¼ cup heavy cream plus 2 tablespoons
  • ⅓ cup grated carrot
  • ¼ cup ground flaxseed
  • 2 tablespoons coconut flour
  • ½ cup erythritol/monk fruit granules
  • ¼ teaspoon baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon Allspice
  • Cream Cheese Frosting

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs and cream together in a small bowl.
  2. Stir in the carrots and set aside.
  3. In another bowl, whisk the dry ingredients together.
  4. Add the wet to the dry and stir until combined.
  5. Grease 2 (8 ounce) quiche style ramekins.
  6. Pour batter evenly into both dishes.
  7. Microwave one at a time for 2½ minutes.
  8. Allow to cool completely before frosting.
  9. Frost individually or stack one on top of the other as you would frost a layer cake.

Notes

Keep refrigerated.

Prep Time: 5 minutes
Cook Time: 5 minutes
Total Time: 10 minutes
Servings: 4
Calories: 158 kcal
Net Carbs: 1.5 grams
