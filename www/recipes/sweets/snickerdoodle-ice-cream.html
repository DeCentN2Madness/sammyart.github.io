GAPS-friendly Snickerdoodle Ice Cream

Snickerdoodle Cookies

Cookie Ingredients

  • 8 Tbsp grass-fed butter, softened
  • 7 egg yolks (or 2 whole eggs)
  • 8 Tbsp honey
  • 4 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 c almond flour
  • 4 Tbsp coconut flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon

Cookie Instructions

  1. Combine the butter, honey, vanilla, and egg in a food processor and process for about 20 seconds, or until combined (will be chunky).
  2. Add the flours, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon then process until combined, about 20 more seconds.
  3. Scoop onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and flatten to about ¼ inch thick.
  4. Bake at 350° for 10 minutes or until lightly golden brown on the outside.
  5. Remove from the oven and let cool on a cooling rack while making the ice cream

Snickerdoodle Ice Cream

Ice Cream Ingredients

  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • ½ c honey
  • 1 ¼ c cultured cream
  • 1 ¼ c full fat coconut milk (no guar gum) (or other preferred milk-almond, cashew, yogurt, or raw milk)*
  • ¾ tsp ground cinnamon

Ice Cream Instructions

  1. In a high-speed blender, combine the egg yolks and honey and blend for 10 seconds, until combined.
  2. Add the cream, milk, vanilla, and cinnamon and blend until combined, about 10 seconds.
  3. Pour into your prepared ice cream maker and follow manufacturer's directions.
  4. Meanwhile, break up your cookies into bite-sized pieces.
  5. When the ice cream has reached a soft serve consistency, stir in the cookies pieces by hand and freeze for at least 8 hours before serving.

Notes

Avoid milks that contain thickening agents. Many of these thickeners, especially carageenan, are inflammatory. They can damage the gut and aggravate digestive issues.
