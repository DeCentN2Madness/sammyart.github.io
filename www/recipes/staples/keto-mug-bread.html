90 Second Keto Bread In A Mug

This amazing keto bread cooks in the microwave for only 90 seconds in a mug! It toasts beautifully and tastes like real bread! No carbs and no gluten! This is the only keto bread recipe you'll ever need!

Serves: 1 serving

Ingredients

  • 1½ tbsp butter
  • 3 tbsp almond flour
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 egg white

Instructions

  1. Put butter in a wide microwavable mug, ramekin, or cereal bowl. Microwave for 10 seconds or until melted.
  2. Add almond flour and baking powder to melted butter and mix until combined. Add the egg white and mix until combined.
  3. Put the mug back in a microwave and microwave on High for 90 seconds.
  4. Remove the keto bread from the mug and slice in half (like an English muffin).
  5. If desired, put the keto bread in the toaster and toast it.

Ingredients

  • 1 Egg
  • 3 tbsp Almond Flour
  • 1 tbsp Mayo
  • ½ tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 tsp Water
  • Pinch of Salt

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, stir together all ingredients until smooth.
  2. Add mixture into microwave safe mug and microwave for 90 seconds, until firm.
  3. Cut in half to form two thick slices.
  4. Toast in a toaster for best results. Enjoy!
